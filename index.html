About Me

I'm Nnenna Jennifer Ajuzieogu

My pronouns are she/her/hers. I prefer to be called Jennifer, as it is much easier to pronounce. I am an international student from Nigeria who came to the U.S in 2014, intending to further my education and gain professional experience. So far, I have a bachelor's degree in public health and a master's degree in business administration. I am currently working on my master's in information technology at Worcester Polytechnic Institute, and it's my second semester. I am a full-time student and work part-time as an assistant manager in a beauty salon. Two years ago, I started weight lifting and realized it helps me keep a clear mind and serves as an escape from reality. With my lack of experience in design, I hope to explore my creativity and make designs that speak to my target audience with the concept of less is more. I'm more business, marketing-oriented, and I have a creative mind.
